PhilHealth receives inquiries from its members regarding membership, eligibility, benefits, and claims processing. Members may ask about becoming a member, updating information, paying premiums, benefits coverage and limits, claims filing, appeal processes, and program and service offerings. These inquiries aim to ensure members understand their rights, access quality healthcare, and navigate the claims process smoothly.

PhilHealth is a government-owned corporation that provides universal health coverage to Filipinos. Members contribute 3.5% of their monthly income, with a minimum of Php 350 and a maximum of Php 2,450. Employers match their employees' contributions. Contributions are tax-deductible and failure to pay on time may result in penalties and coverage suspensions.

Lifetime Members

Members under the Lifetime Member Program (LMP) are those who have reached the age of retirement and have paid at least 120 months' premium. They are granted lifetime coverage without having to pay premium contributions anymore.

Overseas Filipino Workers (OFW)

The Philippine Health Insurance Corporation (PhilHealth) relterates that payment of premium contributions of Overseas Filipino Workers (OFW's) will remain voluntary for the entire duration of the pandemic. OFW's may avail of the flexible payment terms offered by PhilHealth by just paying an initial amount of P2,400 and the balance may be settled in the next 12 months. Meanwhile, repatriated and in distress OFWs must obtain a certification from OWWA to be considered as Indirect Conributors.

Qualified Dependents
